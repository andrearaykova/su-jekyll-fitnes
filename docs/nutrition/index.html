Calorie regulation

Whether your goal is to gain muscle mass, burn fat, improve performance or maintain weight for a particular sport, the most important piece of the food puzzle is to regulate your energy intake properly. People usually have one goal in mind - to reduce fat or increase muscle mass - although everyone wants both. To create an energy deficit or excess, a change in diet should be used, not exercise manipulation.

When people in the industry talk about macronutrients, they mean three types of macronutrients: carbohydrates, proteins and fats. Micronutrients and Water

“Micro” because of quantities: While macro elements are generally calculated in grams, microelements are generally calculated in milligrams (or even smaller units). Think of macronutrients as gas in your car, which gives it energy to power the engine. Micronutrients are oils and lubricants that help keep your car from getting damaged.
